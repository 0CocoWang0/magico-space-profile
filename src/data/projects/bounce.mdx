# Overview

> Bounce Central: social payments, reimagined.

Bounce Central is a product concept that reimagines peer-to-peer payments as a **social experience** rather than a transactional task. Instead of treating payments as isolated actions, the project introduces features like **event-based payments**, **shared moments**, **progress tracking**, and **lightweight gamification** to reduce friction and increase engagement.

Developed as part of a competitive case challenge, where our team placed **3rd overall** (<Green><Link href="https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7434014430511919104/">see the announcement</Link></Green>).
