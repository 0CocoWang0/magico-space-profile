# Overview

> CogSURF: building the communications backbone for a cognitive science community.

I joined **CogSURF** as **Director of Communications** in September 2025. Walked into a lot of manual, fragmented work across email, social, and web, and got to rebuild the system end-to-end so the team could actually reach the community at scale.

# What I Did

- <Green>Transformed a fragmented manual email workflow into an automated, segmented pipeline</Green>, expanding outreach capacity **10× (from 100 to 1,000+ recipients)** while cutting execution time from **hours to minutes**.
- <Green>Drove a 125% increase in brand profile activity and doubled account reach</Green> through a structured content strategy across Instagram and LinkedIn.
- <Green>Redesigned and refreshed the CogSURF website</Green>, bringing the online presence in line with the new brand direction.
- <Green>Owned social media post design and the posting schedule</Green> across Instagram and LinkedIn so the voice stayed consistent week over week.
- <Green>Built email and outreach designs as a system</Green>, so every touchpoint (newsletter, event invite, campaign) felt like the same CogSURF, not four different ones.

# Why It Matters

Communications work is easy to undervalue when it's invisible, and easy to feel when it breaks. The goal was to make the infrastructure boring in the best way: reliable pipelines, a recognizable visual voice, and a team that can focus on the science instead of on the tooling around it.
