# Overview

> Foco — spatial awareness for campuses.

Foco turns existing security cameras and a floor plan into a **live 3D map** of a building. See motion as heat, spot the busiest rooms at a glance, and click any room to reveal its footprint — a new way to understand how space is actually being used.

Built in 24 hours at **JacHacks 2026**, where it placed **2nd overall**.

## The Problem

Campuses are full of cameras, but the feeds live in silos — a grid of shaky tiles that nobody watches. Meanwhile, students and staff constantly ask the same question: _"Where is there still space right now?"_

- <Green>Invisible occupancy:</Green> Camera feeds don't answer "how busy is
  each room?"
- <Green>No spatial context:</Green> A 2D grid doesn't reflect how a building
  is actually laid out.
- <Green>Passive infrastructure:</Green> Existing cameras sit unused for
  anything beyond security.
