# What I Did

- <Green>Owned the core playthrough experience end-to-end</Green>, contributing to **2,000+ completed sessions**, **10+ minute average engagement**, and a **72% organic return rate**.
- <Green>Architected a scalable collectibles system</Green> with structured parameters compatible with generative-AI narration, enabling lightweight branching logic and future extensibility.
- <Green>Hand-drew every collectible in the system</Green>, bringing my artist practice directly into the product so the visual world and the engineering scaffolding stay in sync.

# Role

Product Designer & Engineer, the hybrid seat. I sit in the intersection of design and engineering.
This lets the feel of the product stay intact from sketch to ship, which matters a lot for Trove where how things feel is just as important as what they do.

In this ambiguous space, I worked closely with founder <Green><Link href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/heyohelen/">Helen</Link></Green> and founding engineer <Green><Link href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/davidpotgieter/">David</Link></Green> to carry ideas from brainstorming through to implementation. That tight loop is where most of the product actually gets made.
