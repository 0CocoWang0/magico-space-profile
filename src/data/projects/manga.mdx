When I connect with characters or worlds from games and films, I imagine <Green>new storylines</Green> for them — alternative paths, untold histories, or unseen moments.
These ideas grow into short comics, illustrated sequences, or standalone artworks that expand on the original universe.

## Character Design & Development

This practice of <Green>visual worldbuilding</Green> taught me how to weave character, setting, and narrative together.
It also shaped my love for bridging cultural influences: mixing the expressive clarity of Western cartoons with the layered emotion of Eastern anime/manga.

Over time, I moved toward crafting my own original plots and characters. But I still carry the same instinct to expand, reinterpret, and reimagine — a mindset that shapes how I design interactive experiences, characters, and worlds.
